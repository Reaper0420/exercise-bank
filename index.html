Ascending (Light → Heavy)

Add load every set as reps drop. Warms the pattern in, peaks at your heaviest work. The default pyramid.

12 reps · light
10 reps · +weight
8 reps · +weight
6 reps · heaviest
Try: Goblet Squat 12-10-8-6

Descending / Reverse (Heavy → Light)

Warm up thoroughly first, then hit your heaviest set fresh and strip load as fatigue builds. Best strength stimulus per set.

6 reps · heaviest
8 reps · −weight
10 reps · −weight
12 reps · light, to near-failure
Try: DB Floor Press 6-8-10-12
